How It Works

Gameplay Notes

  • Move the blue player block to collect the gold coins.
  • Difficulty increases with score through faster enemies and additional threats.
  • Shield pickups let you survive one collision and keep the run going.
  • The game ends when an enemy touches the player while the shield is offline.
  • Press Enter after a loss to restart from the keyboard.
This version adds better progression and recovery mechanics so the project feels more complete without losing its original simplicity.
