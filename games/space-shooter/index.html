How It Works

Gameplay Notes

  • Move the ship horizontally and hold fire to clear incoming asteroids.
  • Every few points, the run levels up with faster asteroids and additional threats.
  • Your ship can survive multiple hits, with a short recovery window after impact.
  • Rapid-fire pickups temporarily upgrade the ship into a stronger dual-shot weapon.
  • Press Enter after a loss to restart from the keyboard.
